What GreenOps Is

GreenOps is the discipline of making digital consumption visible, governable, and optimisable.

What This Course Is

Most of what you have seen on digital sustainability is either too vague, too preachy, or completely detached from how IT actually works. Organisations respond with glossy targets or produce annual reports. Very few build the operational discipline that changes day-to-day decisions.

This course is the practical alternative — engineering-first, grounded in governance and evidence, built for everyone who makes decisions about what technology to buy, build, run, or retire.

Course framing

What this course is — and isn't.

✓
A practical foundation in operational reality

Consumption discipline grounded in how infrastructure, cloud, software, and supply chains actually work.

✗
A climate science lecture or activism script

Not a session on how to feel better about technology. The starting point is waste, not ideology.

✓
Grounded in engineering, governance, cost, and risk

Connects sustainability to the decisions technology leaders and practitioners already own.

✗
An annual report exercise

Reporting is a starting point. This course is about what comes after it.

✓
Built for practitioners and leaders across technology

From infrastructure engineers to CIOs. Three tracks, each with its own entry point.

✗
A set of aspirational targets

Targets without operational discipline are communications. This course builds the discipline.

Navigation

How to use this course.

01
Start at your track

Each track is a coherent entry point. Track 1 is recommended for anyone new to the domain. Tracks 2 and 3 assume familiarity with the foundational concepts.

02
Work each module independently

Every module is self-contained. Each ends with a knowledge check and a Pause and Reflect prompt. Don't skip these — they are where application happens.

03
Use the accreditation test to confirm

The Practitioner Accreditation Test is available after completing all modules. It tests application, not recall — and confirms competency in the full discipline.

🏅

GreenOps Practitioner — Foundation Accreditation

On completing all 14 modules and passing the accreditation test, you receive the GreenOps Practitioner Foundation credential from the TBM Council. This demonstrates competency across the full GreenOps discipline — not just awareness of the topic.
